s01

I’m Leona, a creative social media manager and content creator with a strong sense for beauty, storytelling, and connection. Over the past few years, I’ve managed social media accounts, produced and edited media, and helped wellness brands and small businesses communicate their vision in a clear and engaging way. My background in art and community projects makes me especially passionate about creating content that feels authentic and inspiring, while still being strategic and evidence-based.
